Galactic Guardian
	A bullet hell game that takes place in space. Fight your way through as many waves of enemies and bosses as you can.

Controls:
	WASD: movement
	T (or space): Dash (3 second cooldown)
	Y: Fire Charge Laser

Installation Instructions:
	Unzip the file and then just boot the "bullet hell.exe" found in the windows folder (the only supported OS at the moment)
